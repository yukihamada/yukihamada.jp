# Try Recording in Airplane Mode

After downloading Koe, turn on airplane mode first.

Then enable Sound Memory and say something.

Transcription works normally.

No internet connection. That's everything you need to know about Koe.

## Your Voice Never Leaves the Device

Sound Memory continuously records in the background, auto-transcribes with whisper.cpp, and makes everything full-text searchable.

"What did they say in that meeting?" "What was that idea from yesterday?" — it catches everything. Recordings are segmented every 30 seconds and auto-deleted after 7 days.

**Your data stays on this iPhone until you decide to "send" it.**

Copy, share, Mac sync — all require your action. Nothing ever leaves on its own.

## It Flows to Your Mac Automatically

If Koe is running on a Mac on the same WiFi, transcribed text from your iPhone appears at your Mac's cursor position automatically.

Leave your iPhone on the table during a meeting. Meeting notes flow into your Mac.

This also stays on the local network. Never touches the internet.

## The Photos App, but for Audio

Photos are saved to your camera roll the moment you tap the shutter. Nobody asks "do you want to save this?"

Audio should work the same way.

With Sound Memory on, every sound is automatically recorded, transcribed, and searchable later. The Photos app for audio. That's what Koe is building toward.
